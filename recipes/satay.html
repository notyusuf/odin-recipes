Satay Recipe

Description

Satay is a dish of skewered, grilled meat or seafood that originated in Indonesia. The meat is typically cut into small pieces and marinated in a mixture of spices and herbs before being grilled over an open flame. Satay can be made with a variety of meats, including chicken, beef, lamb, pork, and seafood. It is often served with a dipping sauce, such as peanut sauce, chili sauce, or sweet soy sauce.

Satay is a popular dish in Indonesia and can be found throughout the country. It is also popular in other Southeast Asian countries, such as Malaysia, Singapore, and Thailand. Satay has become increasingly popular in other parts of the world, and can now be found in many restaurants and street food stalls.
